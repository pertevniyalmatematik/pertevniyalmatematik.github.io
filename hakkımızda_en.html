About us

Who are we?

We are the students of Pertevniyal who do not see mathematics merely as a subject or an academic pursuit, but as the purest form of understanding, thinking, and questioning the universe. This club is the starting point of a journey that goes beyond numbers. Our goal is not just to learn formulas or solve problems; it is to improve our way of thinking, deepen our analytical perspective, and grow our mathematical awareness together. Behind every question lies a new realm of thought; behind every proof, a new discovery awaits. In this club, we come together to experience the excitement of exploring mathematics together‚Äîto discuss, think, and create. Because we know that mathematics is not a result, but a way of thinking. And our greatest passion is to build, live, and share this mindset together.

What do we aim for?

Our aim is to redefine mathematics not merely as a school subject, but as a fundamental way of thinking, questioning, and understanding. In this direction, we strive to create and share by publishing our own mathematics magazine; to draw inspiration by exploring the lives of distinguished Turkish and international mathematicians; and to transform mathematics into a living discipline through organized talks and discussions. At the same time, by participating in academic research competitions, we aim to become active contributors to science and to provide a guiding resource for students who wish to pursue further studies in this field.
